Nurbits started as an experiment: what if learning neuroscience felt like being a sound engineer at a live show?

The core mechanic maps neuron behavior — excitation, inhibition, signal propagation — onto a circuit board of chips. Connecting them correctly unlocks layers of the song. Get it wrong and the music falls apart. It's puzzle design that teaches without ever feeling like a lesson.

I built the entire game in Unity/C# — the puzzle engine, the Creative Mode editor, the online sharing infrastructure, and the sprite pipeline. Shipping on Steam (Windows, Mac, Linux) with 18 achievements was the capstone of a project that started in a classroom and ended on a digital storefront.
